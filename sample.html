Four Ethnic Dressed to Slay the Diwali Look

Diwali is the festival of lights, celebrated with grandeur, zest, zeal and enthusiasm. The occasion has survived since ancient times as it heralds a few days of relief from the monotonous routined life. Family get-togethers, bursting crackers, distributing sweets, spreading smiles add a tint of euphoria to this auspicious occasion. To amplify the feels of festivity people embellish their houses, decorate them with colorful lights, candles and diyas. The sky seems to be studded with flying lanterns, and adorned with different patterns of fireworks. To experience the exuberance to the brim, people clad themselves in beautiful and ravishing ethnic wears.

The Joy of Dressing Up

Diwali is the opportunity for you to doll up in magnificent ethnics. Diwali may be less than a one-week event, but the plans and excitement of dressing up start bubbling about a month prior to the occasion. With a plethora of options most women choose magnificent ethnics. Online shopping, endless store hopping, do not seem to dwindle their ardour. Matching ornaments, and the perfect make up make the ladies look gorgeous.

Whatever you choose to wear, it should carry a perfect combination of safety and style. Delve into the wide range of Kreeva’s clannish collection, moulded with the finest raw materials, and a variety of colors and patterns.

Sarees

Sarees are correctly called to be six yards of grace. Sarees when draped, and coupled with the perfect blouse, append couture and elegance to your beauty. From chiffon to Chanderi, handloom to Banarasi, georgette to ruffled sarees, you can slay the look with any one of these. It is an attire that blends both dignity and style.

Sarees have evolved from being a traditional clothing to a chic fashion wear. They have successfully managed to bag the top position in every Indian woman’s heart. The eye-catching and elegant, sartorial and stunning garment makes you stand out in the crowd.

You can rely on sarees for every occasion. Bengali tant or embroidered Kanjivarams will accompany you in pujas. Silk sarees offer a subtle bling in family gatherings. Banglori Malai brings in a mixture of trend and tradition. Georgette sarees are an ideal for a classic chic evening, whereas nude shades of chiffon leave behind a tantalising effect. You can steal the thunder of the party by draping a bright and enigmatic net saree, or win hearts with a smile and a simple digital silk.

Salwar Kameez

Salwar Kameez is the ideal attire for an event where tradition encounters trends. It has managed to survive the long run, and occupy a place in every woman’s wardrobe. Diwali and salwars share an amazing kinship. The traditional wear gets its name from the top (salwar) and the bottom (kameez). This is often paired with matching or contrasting dupattas to step up the game.

Satin, chanderi, linen or cotton salwars are a perfect balance of style, and comfort. For the main Diwali functions you could sport ethnics from fabrics like raw silk, brocades, georgettes, lace, net or embroidered work. You can couple light colored tops with complementary bottoms, or choose traditional glass work on your salwars.

Some prefer big heavy borders, whereas some opt for light or medium bordered attires and pair them with semi heavy jewellery. In recent times, women have gone all hearts over the fabric velvet, as it is a perfect blend of chic and class. Be it a call for a lavish, gorgeous look during the main events, or simple placid look during the morning activities, salwars will respond in the most satiable way.

Lehenga Choli

Women from almost every Indian community awe over charismatic and stunning lehenga cholis. No matter how fashion forward you become, you are bound to fall in love with raiment. Also known as ghagra choli, or chaniya choli, it has ruled women’s hearts for the past few decades. Vibrant lehenga cholis are full ankle-length flared skirts, with intricate embroideries, and neat pleats. Decking up in these voluminous lehengas, cholis, and dupattas will glam you up.

Some lehengas are accentuated with high-quality fabric base, some are adorned with mirrors, and zari, some embellished with sequins, sparkles and embroideries. A-line lehengas with broad flares and numerous pleats, sharara-cut lehengas designs like palzoos, pandelled lehengas with horizontal panels of fabric, mermaid lehengas to flaunt your curves, flared lehengas for the ‘lehenga-twirl’ photoshoot will fulfil all your desires.

Anarkali

Anarkalis have ruled the rack for quite a long period of time, and Indian women have knitted an emotional attachment with it. No matter how many anarkalis you own, and how many times you have worn it, your love for this royal robe remains the same. From cotton to zaris, Anarkalis have dominated the field since times, when other entrants had not even stepped in. Diwali without Anarkalis is difficult to imagine.

Anarkali is the traditional wear that never seems to fall out of style, They are long, voluminous, and the A-line cut starts above their hips. This traditional attire, fit and flare, with a variety of cuts, designs, prints and embroideries clad you in a sensational party look. Wearing big jhumkas with gorgeous Anarkalis will add vigour to your ethnicity.

To add a tinge of uniqueness, designers have experimented with Anarkalis and introduced new styles. The obsession over designer anarkalis with fabric base and intricate embroidery is ever increasing. If you desire to blend your traditional look with a contemporary appeal front open A-line Kurta is what you are looking for. The craze for Anarkalis has also focussed on flared Anarkalis and asymmetric Anarkalis.


Building your Diwali wardrobe,sounds interesting and is something almost every Indian woman looks forward to. However, it isn’t an easy affair, and demands patience. Safety and style, class and charm should be equally prioritised. Kreeva’s exclusive collection, introduces you to an array of ethnic wears, with a broad variety of fabrics, patterns, and styles. From draping a saree, to twirling in your lehenga, from flaunting your dupatta, to walking in a gorgeous long dress, pick up any look of your choice and add color and delight to these festive times. This Diwali, unravel the Desi girl in you with your favorite attire, and a smile.

You find it cute but peep out of the window to see, scrambled skyscrapers, and UFOs that have ploughed the heart of your city! Scared you jump out of your bed, to realize it was just a bad dream.

All of this could be true. Life could be ulterior, and probably all we see is a speck in the chaotic universe. Bigelow Ranch, The Devil’s Sea, and The Bermuda Triangle might be the peaks of an iceberg.

People have always wanted to quench their thirst regarding the cosmos, galaxies, celestial bodies, and the driving forces of the universe. There have been plenty of stories, theories, concepts, etc. based on space warfare, adventure, and interplanetary battles. All these come under the sub-genre space opera.

Recursive dreams, as in the movie Inception could be the ultimate reality. Everything around us seems to be real, but that’s because we do not question or introspect. The entire existence could be similar to the adventure in the movie Gravity, where humans explore space. With the overexploitation of resources, we may soon be in the shoes of Na’vi of Pandora in the movie Avatar. Na’vi were the natives of Pandora(The Moon), whose existence was threatened due to the expansion of human mining colonies. On squandering our resources, we would concoct to steal it from others, just like Planet Spcaeball tried to steal air from Druidia (in movie Spaceballs). And, finally, we’ll be left with no other option other than moving to a new planet, as in the movie Interstellar. The dilapidated view of the movie 2012 will be the final fate.

Thus, life is no less than a movie, and we humans are just a mere fractal of this screenplay. Resources are ephemeral. Without realizing we have become a part of this space opera universe. As members of the cast, it is in our hands whether we choose the movie to play, pause or stop!!

SWOT ANALYSIS OF TAJ HOTELS

The TAJ HOTELS is a chain of luxury hotels and a subsidiary of the Indian Hotels Company Limited, owned by the TATA group, with its headquarters in Mumbai. As of 2018, the company operates a total of 100 hotels and hotel-resorts, with 84 across India and 16 in other countries, including Bhutan, Malaysia, Maldives, Nepal, South Africa, Sri Lanka, UAE, UK, USA and Zambia. Two hotels of the Taj group, namely Rambagh Palace in Jaipur and the Taj Mahal Palace & Tower in Mumbai, were ranked in 2013 by Condé Nast Traveler among its "Top 100 Hotels and Resorts in the World". Taj’s spirit of hospitality is always at the peak.

Hospitality is the service of welcoming and receiving guests, looking after them, their needs, likings, which also include reception and entertainment. It creates a bond between the host and the guest. Hospitality sector is an umbrella term, under which comes event planning, event managing, travel and tourism, sailing trips or cruise, food and accommodation services.

To develop a business be it in hospitality sector or in any other field, an analysis is very important. SWOT is one such mode of analysis that helps to assess the opportunities, advantages, disadvantages, gains and losses and keeping these in mind one can implement ideas for the company to grow in an organized way.

SWOT is an acronym for strengths, weaknesses, opportunities, and threats. By taking these elements into consideration for a particular business, one can witness better outcomes.

    STRENGTHS:
    This refers to the advantages or beneficial aspects of the business. This includes the features that make your company unique and different from other competitors, efficient staff, technology, location, brand reputation etc.
  • Taj is a heritage and Taj Hotels have got a good reputation for more than 100 years, which tend to attract more tourists. It is considered to be the most premium hotel chain in India. It has been one of the leading hospitality sectors of India for many years now.Travelling will never stop completely. People will go from one place to another be it for business, office purposes, education, or vacation. This leads to a market for the hospitality sectors, like the Taj.
  • Their hotels have ample facilities for guests like rooms, spas, jacuzzi, multi-cuisine restaurants, swimming pools, pubs, resto cafes, fitness centers, and banquet halls.Taj Khazana has been known to be a custodian of Indian tradition. Celebrating the boundless creativity of Indian craftsmen, the chain aims to revive traditional art, craft and textiles of this beautiful country and nurture the talent of master artisans and weavers who are sustained through this venture.1
  • The Jiva Spa at Taj is a special attraction, it helps one awaken their inner self through unique healing, wellness and beauty secrets. Taj Hotels Palaces Resorts and Safaris, conduct events and programs like a significant meeting, a crucial conference, a much-awaited get-together, or a wedding.
  • One of the positive aspects of the Taj hotels is that it is available for people of almost all financial backgrounds. The different segments are- Taj Vivanta for the upper upscale segment, Taj the luxury segment, Gateway the upscale and Ginger the economy segment, which shows that Taj Hotels cater to needs of all irrespective of economic status.
  • One specialty of the Taj groups is the customer loyalty program where a customer can collect points for his stay in the hotel and get upgraded to a higher-class segment of the four above mentioned segments.
  • Taj Hotels warmly welcome guests of all religions, cultures from every nook and corner of the world. They cater to the different needs of guests of different choices, likings, habits. The Taj research team has noted down 155 costumer service touch points and each of these points have got separate service division. This sector creates job opportunities for different kinds of people both directly and indirectly. Taj hotels have started the Taj Residency, a business class hotel, for the business class people who travel.
    WEAKNESS:
    This refers to the factors that pull down the reputation of the company, and hinder its growth. This includes strong competitors, lack of proper technologies and amenities, resource limitations, poor online reviews, and a website that is not updated.
  • Since, it is highly dependent on tourists and tourism, there is seasonal dependency also.Tax rates on hotels vary from country to country. But governments usually impose a very high tax rates\ on these famous hotels and restaurants. That’s why they have no other choice, but to charge more from their customers.

  • Increasing competition from Oberoi, Marriot, Sheraton, Radisson, Hilton, Wyndham Worldwide, and Intercontinental Hotels can be considered as threats.

  • Currently, the pandemic of coronavirus (COVID-19) has caused a worldwide locked down. Only stores of essential commodities are open for the public to buy things. Such pandemic has affected the hospitality industry like hotels and restaurants. People are unable to go to work and are living on their savings. Maintaining social distancing is important. Sleep Well and Wake Up as a Beauty Queen!

Wake up! It's time to be even more beautiful! What is the first thing that strikes your mind when you hear 'beauty'? It's probably fashion, glamour, and flawless skin! Beauty is not just about your external appearance. It is also the way you present yourself. Greeting people with a vibrant smile and twinkling eyes is way more attractive than a somber yawn and sleepy eyes.

Can sleep make you a beauty queen?

Sleep nourishes the brain, body, and skin. According to Rebecca S Robbins, sleep eliminates about 60% of brain toxins. Natural beauty is unmatched. What's the most beautiful apparel you can choose? It is your smile. To smile, you need to sleep properly and keep your mind fresh.

Sleep deprivation and dwindling beauty.

Lack of sleep affects the functioning of appetite hormones, thus causing frequent cravings. Sleep deprivation leads to fatigue and discourages your desire to workout, which intensifies weight gain. Insufficient sleep disrupts your immune system and is linked to inflammation and depression. Reduced sleep also affects your physical appearance.

Recent definitions of beauty demand a slim and fit figure. But are you willing to embrace wrinkled skin, spots on your face, dark circles, and swollen eyes? If not, proper sleep is the only key. Long periods of deprived sleep can eventually lead to damaged skin. Lack of sleep affects your emotional and social interactions. It makes you short-tempered, and your inner beauty fades away as well.

What is the solution?

Beauty naps enhance your glow but are they miracles? No. You need proper, consistent sleep hours. Soft bed linens, comfortable pillows, and cozy mattresses offer sleeping solutions. Prioritize your sleep and skin, and wake up with a glam 'I got up like this' look.

Say goodbye to interrupted sleep schedules and wake up with an irresistible smile with Sleep to Beauty.
